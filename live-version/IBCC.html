Online Trainings

IBCC

The Introduction to Biblical Counseling Certificate is awarded to those who complete 4 modules as given below. Each module consists of 12-13 lessons which is conducted on Zoom over a period of 7 weeks with 2 classes per week.

1. How People Change

A Biblical model of human behaviour based on the heart. Participants will learn to apply fundamental Biblical truths to their own daily lives, identify areas where they need to change and access resources of the Spirit to apply the change to their own lives first.

Know More

2. Instruments in the Redeemer’s Hands

Learn how to counsel the way Jesus did! The model is seen in 4 processes called Love-Know-Speak-Do, where one learns how to listen well, empathize, ask good questions and speaking the truth in love to help those who struggle with various issues.

Know More

3. Health and Healing

Learning more about Suffering, grieving, death, dealing with depression, fear, anxiety, anger, addictions, mental health etc.

Know More

4. Marriage & Parenting

A biblical understanding and practical aspects of marriage including communication, relational trends, conflict management etc as well understanding how to shepherd your children’s hearts instead of manipulating them!
