BCTI - Biblical Counseling Trust of India

BCTI is a movement established to support the Church in Hindi-speaking regions of North India to respond to the challenges of Christian living in the 21st century. We do this by building the capacity of the church to become a caring community through biblically based training and resources on issues of everyday living.

Dedicated Personal Counselors

We are living in troubled times and the journey we are going through, is often unkind. It is God’s desire that we discover the power of His comfort, healing and restoration in the shelter of genuine, authentic and caring relationships.

Dedicated Courses

BCTI trains pastors & leaders to be compassionate counselors. Our courses are offered online and can also be adapted for in person training needs, both in English and Hindi.

Biblical Counselling Service For You

Couple Counseling

Couples counseling, also known as marriage counseling, is a type of counseling for intimate partners. It involves exploration of any conflicts between the partners.

Individual counseling

Individual ling is a personal, one-to-one counseling space for a person to interact with the counselor to explore and address personal concerns.

Pre-Marital Counseling

This will help you and your partner to prepare for marriage, especially by exploring into ones beliefs and opininions.

Family Counseling

Family counseling, or family therapy, aims to address psychological, behavioral, and emotional issues that cause family problems. Family members will work with a therapist.
