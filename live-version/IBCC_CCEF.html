Online Trainings

BCTI-CCEF

BCTI is working in partnership with Christian Counseling and Educational Foundation (CCEF), which is an organization is based in America, to offer biblical counselling courses online to people residing in India. BCTI online training is designed for Christians who want to grow in biblical wisdom; learning to see life as God sees it and change as God does it. We have trainings both in English and Hindi.

Some students simply want to be better equipped to help friends and family with their struggles. Other students want to go on to work as counsellors. BCTI online training is suitable for both students! Our online students are youth workers, doctors, nurses, pastors, pastor’s wives, teachers, parents, other professionals, missionaries, NGO workers. They come from all over India. They find taking an online class offers great flexibility, as they are able to watch lectures and do assignments according to their schedule.

Please note that neither BCTI nor CCEF are accrediting institutions.

Initially BCTI students can work toward a Formation Certificate. This will be awarded by ‘BCTI in partnership with CCEF’. The details of the courses that make up this certificate are outlined below:

Course Lecturer
Dynamics of Biblical Change Dr David Powlison
Helping Relationships Dr Ed Welch
BCTI 2-Day Online Residential BCTI Faculty
Each course costs Rs. 7000/-

Each course typically runs over a semester. A semester is usually 11 weeks, 10 weeks of class including a final exam/ paper due, and one-week mid-semester break (often a good time to catch up on readings if you are behind).

Once BCTI students have completed this certificate they will be eligible to register for additional courses directly with CCEF (BCTI will be facilitating registrations). BCTI students can then work toward CCEF’s certificate structure (click on the link to see details).
