Welcome to Rainbow Consultancy

About Rainbow Consultancy

Rainbow Consultancy is a consortium of independent professional consultants with a commitment to provide quality training, evaluation and documentation services to our clients. Operating since 1993 we have successfully completed more than 300 assignments including, Research Evaluations and project related consultancies in India, Nepal, Philippines, Thailand, Sweden, Liberia, England, Nairobi and Dubai. Rainbow Consultancy provides a spectrum of business services to clients. We conduct need-based trainings for top, middle level and grass root level workers of national and international participants. Focussing on NGO management, strategic planning, systems, policy and organizational change, we facilitate OD programs and provide assistance in strategic planning, monitoring and documentation. Overseas funding partners avail our services to monitor or evaluate the projects they supported. We envision value based professionalism in organizational management. From a small venture, Rainbow Consultancy has now facilitated international training programs on behalf of various National Organizations

What we do

We assist Organizations, NGO’s in documentation which includes developing project proposal, writing case studies, project implementation report. We do research on important issues of community concerns. The consultants have wide experience of doing project evaluations which may be either mid-term or final project evaluation. Our team organizes need based, tailor made training for project implementers or organizational leaders (leadership development and governance). Over the years we have facilitated organizational development (OD) including strategic planning of varieties of organizations. We have designed assessment tools like QIMP (to assess the organizational culture and quality / type of management in the organizations), SHG – level assessment (SHG self reliance and sustainable level), and assessing the self esteem of ground level staff.
